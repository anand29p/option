## рЯТ∞ Income Tax on Options Profits

Options trading P&L is classified as:
- **Speculative Business Income** if options are not hedged (most retail cases)
- **Non-speculative Business Income** if proper hedging/trading business

### Tax Slabs (FY 2024-25, New Regime)
| Income Slab | Tax Rate |
|------------|---------|
| Up to вВє3,00,000 | Nil |
| вВє3,00,001 вАУ вВє6,00,000 | 5% |
| вВє6,00,001 вАУ вВє9,00,000 | 10% |
| вВє9,00,001 вАУ вВє12,00,000 | 15% |
| вВє12,00,001 вАУ вВє15,00,000 | 20% |
| Above вВє15,00,000 | 30% |

> **Note:** Income tax is calculated annually on **net profits** and is NOT deducted per trade by the bot. Only transaction-level charges (STT, brokerage, etc.) are deducted per trade.

### ITR Filing
- File **ITR-3** for F&O trading income
- Maintain proper books (trade logs generated by this bot can help)
- Losses from F&O can be carried forward for **8 years** (non-speculative)
- Consult a CA for your specific tax situation
